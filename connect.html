A bit about me outside of recovery

I roof full time from April to December. In the winter I like to use my spare time on the computer, read about tech, get into novels and series, and do a lot of self-directed learning. Fitness is a big part of my life — always has been, even when everything else was falling apart.

I'm not trying to be a recovery influencer or build a brand. I just think lived experience is underused and I'd rather put what I know somewhere useful than keep it to myself.
