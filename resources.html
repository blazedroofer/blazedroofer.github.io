Detox & Withdrawal · Ottawa

Medical detox is the only safe move with today's supply. Don't white-knuckle it.

Ottawa Withdrawal Management Centre
Main intake for Ottawa. 20 beds, co-ed, 16+. Call first — they'll tell you if there's a bed and walk you through what to bring. Best window is early morning.
(613) 241-1525
Detox · Beds
RAAM Clinic — The Royal
Rapid Access Addiction Medicine. No referral needed. Walk-in or check in online — they assess you and start treatment (Suboxone, methadone, counselling) the same day. Often the fastest real door.
(613) 722-6521 ×6508
